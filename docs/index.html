Wenliang joined Soochow University in 2013. Prior to joining Soochow University, he was a research scientist in the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R) of Singapore from 2011 to 2013. From 2005 to 2010, he worked as an expert researcher in NICT, Japan.

Before that, he was a student at NLPLab of Northeastern University, China since July 1999. He received Master degree in Computational Linguistics in Mar. 2002 and received PhD degree in Computational Linguistics in Mar. 2005. The PhD thesis advisers were Prof. Yao Tianshun and Zhu Jingbo.
